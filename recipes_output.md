## Dish Name: French Fries

### 1. Time & Difficulty  
- Prep Time: 10 minutes  
- Cook Time: 20 minutes  
- Total Time: 30 minutes  
- Difficulty: Easy  

### 2. Nutrition Facts (Per Serving)  
- Calories: 196  
- Protein (g): 2  
- Carbohydrates (g): 23  
- Fat (g): 11  

### 3. Ingredients  
- 2 large russet potatoes  
- 2 cups vegetable oil (for frying)  
- 1/2 teaspoon salt  

### 4. Cooking Steps  
1. Peel and cut the potatoes into 1/4-inch thick strips.  
2. Soak the cut potatoes in cold water for 10 minutes, then drain and pat dry.  
3. Heat the vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 325°F (165°C).  
4. Fry the potatoes in batches for 3-4 minutes until soft but not browned. Remove and drain on paper towels.  
5. Increase the oil temperature to 375°F (190°C).  
6. Fry the potatoes again in batches for 2-3 minutes until golden and crispy.  
7. Drain on paper towels and sprinkle with salt.  

### 5. Allergen Warnings  
- None  

### 6. Dietary Labels  
- Vegetarian  
- Vegan (if using plant-based oil)  
- Gluten-Free

## Dish Name: Hamburger

### 1. Time & Difficulty  
- Prep Time: 10 minutes  
- Cook Time: 10 minutes  
- Total Time: 20 minutes  
- Difficulty: Easy  

### 2. Nutrition Facts (Per Serving)  
- Calories: 350  
- Protein (g): 25  
- Carbohydrates (g): 30  
- Fat (g): 15  

### 3. Ingredients  
- 1 lb ground beef (80% lean)  
- 1 tsp salt  
- 1/2 tsp black pepper  
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder  
- 4 hamburger buns  
- 4 slices cheddar cheese (optional)  
- Lettuce, tomato, onion (optional, for topping)  
- Ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise (optional, for condiments)  

### 4. Cooking Steps  
1. Preheat a grill or skillet to medium-high heat.  
2. In a bowl, mix ground beef, salt, pepper, and garlic powder until combined.  
3. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape into patties.  
4. Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.  
5. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking.  
6. Toast the hamburger buns lightly on the grill or skillet.  
7. Assemble the burgers by placing the patties on the buns and adding desired toppings and condiments.  

### 5. Allergen Warnings  
- Contains gluten (buns)  
- Contains dairy (cheese, if used)  

### 6. Dietary Labels  
- High-protein  
- Can be made gluten-free (use gluten-free buns)  
- Can be made dairy-free (omit cheese)
